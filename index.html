Zoey Walker

Zoey Walker is a 24 year-old American actress. She was born and raised in Reno, Nevada. She attended the University of Nevada, Reno as well as Gallaudet University and is fluent in American Sign Language. Zoey has starred in student films, web series, and commercials.
